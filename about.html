At Riyanshi Pie, we take our reputation seriously. So much so, that other people have taken notice. Check out a few of the people that love us and then give us a try. We think you'll love us too.

Our History

For few years, Riyanshi has been baking the most delicious pies at her home. In 2015, we opened our first store in Mirzapur (Uttar Pradesh) in a cosy little street near the Grand Market. People from all over the city fell in love with the soft cakes, heavenly cheese cakes, spicy apple pies... The store became a success in no time.

In 2017, no less than 100 pies were sold every day and Riyanshi's Pie Shop moved into a larger building. After receiving many requests, it became clear that our next step was making it possible to order pies from the comfort of your own home and from anywhere in the city. You are now looking at the result of this: our very own webstore, making it possible to order Riyanshi's delicious pies whenever you feel like it!

Our Production

Most pies use only shortcrust pastry but we find that the luxurious taste of a puff pastry top adds to the flavour and experience of eating our pies.

Our pies are always baked to order so are never more than a few days old! You won’t find our pies sitting on supermarket shelves for weeks.

We follow the same philosophy with our Sweet pies using large chunks of fresh fruit to give a tasty filling always ‘crammed to the crust’ inside a shortcrust pastry base and a puff pastry top.

What makes our pies special

Riyanshi Pie Shop is a farm-to-table bakery cafe specializing in sweet and savory pies made with local and organic ingredients.

We take the idea of ‘farm to table’ seriously and purchase many of our ingredients directly from farmers and other producers.”

We make weekly trips to the San Rafael Civic Center Farmers Market to buy fresh and organic fruits and vegetables for the pies and gets regular deliveries of produce, eggs, dairy and more from local producers and suppliers.

Riyanshi Pie Shop changes its menu seasonally so there is always something new to try.
